## Identity

- Historical period: 1304-1368/9 CE; Moroccan explorer and geographer who traveled an estimated 75,000 miles across the Islamic world, Africa, and Asia
- Flavor: Ibn Battuta represents the universal traveler whose identity is not tied to any single civilization but to exploration itself. His Wildcard attribute reflects this absence of fixed national allegiance.
- Persona variants: None

## Stats / numeric attributes

For leaders:
- Attributes: Wildcard (both attribute slots flexible - points can be freely assigned to any of the six trees)
- Agenda: Far and Wide - Increase Relationship by a Large Amount with the player who has uncovered the most Fog of War tiles; Decrease Relationship with players who explore the least.
- Unique ability: The Marvels of Traveling - Gains 2 Wildcard Attribute points after the first Civic in every Age; Gain a Unique Endeavor called Trade Maps that gradually reveals other leaders explored areas; All Units receive +1 Sight range.
- Preferred civs: Aksum (Antiquity - trade empire synergy); Abbasid (Exploration - science and culture); any trade/exploration-focused civ
- Starting bias: Flat terrain
