## Identity

- Historical period: 1729-1796; Empress of Russia, longest-ruling female leader of Russia
- Flavor: Catherine is associated with Enlightenment patronage, the arts, and territorial expansion into tundra frontiers. Her design rewards tundra colonization and Great Work curation.
- Persona variants: None

## Stats / numeric attributes

For leaders:
- Attributes: Cultural (primary), Scientific (secondary)
- Agenda: Dusha - Decrease Relationship by a Medium Amount with the player holding the most Great Works; Increase Relationship by a Medium Amount with the player holding the fewest Great Works. Only activates if Catherine has at least one Great Work.
- Unique ability: Star of the North - +2 Culture per Age on displayed Great Works; Buildings with Great Work slots gain an additional slot; Cities settled in Tundra gain Science equal to 25% of their Culture per turn.
- Preferred civs: Maurya (Antiquity); Russia (Modern - doubles down on culture and science)
- Starting bias: Tundra

## Notes / uncertainty

The 25% Culture-to-Science conversion in tundra cities is confirmed from Fextralife. Extra Great Work slot per building confirmed from Fextralife.
