## Identity

- Historical period: 742-814 CE; King of the Franks and first Holy Roman Emperor
- Flavor: Charlemagne unified much of Western Europe through military campaigns and Christian administration. His design centers on cavalry power during celebrations and military-building adjacency bonuses.
- Persona variants: None

## Stats / numeric attributes

For leaders:
- Attributes: Militaristic (primary), Scientific (secondary)
- Agenda: The Golden Shepherd - Increase Relationship by a Medium Amount (or Small if tied) with players who trigger the most Celebrations; Decrease Relationship by a Medium Amount with players triggering the fewest.
- Unique ability: Father of Europe - Military and Science Buildings receive a Happiness adjacency for Quarters; Gain 2 free Cavalry Units when entering a Celebration; +5 Combat Strength for Cavalry Units during a Celebration.
- Preferred civs: Maurya (Antiquity); Maya (Antiquity alternative); Norman (Exploration - cavalry synergy); Mongolian (Exploration); Prussia (Modern)
- Starting bias: Rivers
