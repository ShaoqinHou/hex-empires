## Identity

- Historical period: 1757-1834; Marquis de Lafayette, French military officer who served in both the American Revolutionary War and the French Revolution
- Flavor: Lafayette embodies revolutionary idealism and the bridge between Old World aristocracy and New World democracy. His design rewards tradition-stacked governments, distant-lands settlement, and sharing diplomatic benefits.
- Persona variants: None

## Stats / numeric attributes

For leaders:
- Attributes: Cultural (primary), Diplomatic (secondary)
- Agenda: French Quarters - Increase Relationship with players who have the most fully-built urban districts; Decrease Relationship with players who have the fewest urban districts.
- Unique ability: Hero of Two Worlds - Gain a Unique Endeavor called Reform that grants an additional Social Policy slot (and also grants one to the supporting leader); +1 Combat Strength for every Tradition (not Policy) slotted in the Government; +2 Culture and Happiness per Age in Settlements, doubled in Distant Lands.
- Preferred civs: France (Exploration/Modern); America (Modern)
- Starting bias: Coast
