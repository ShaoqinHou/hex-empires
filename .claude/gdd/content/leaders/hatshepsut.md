## Identity

- Historical period: c. 1507-1458 BCE; fifth pharaoh of the Eighteenth Dynasty of Egypt, one of the most prolific builders in ancient Egyptian history
- Flavor: Hatshepsut is famous for trade expeditions to Punt and her extensive building program including Deir el-Bahari. Her design rewards imported resources, navigable river construction, and Wonder building.
- Persona variants: None

## Stats / numeric attributes

For leaders:
- Attributes: Cultural (primary), Economic (secondary)
- Agenda: Wonders of Iteru - Decrease Relationship by a Medium Amount when you build more Wonders than Hatshepsut; Increase Relationship by a Medium Amount when you build fewer Wonders.
- Unique ability: God's Wife of Amun - Adds Culture for every imported Resource; 15% Increased Production towards constructing Buildings and Wonders in Cities adjacent to Navigable Rivers.
- Preferred civs: Egypt (Antiquity - canonical pairing; all bonuses synergize); Abbasid (Exploration - Wonder construction enhancement)
- Starting bias: Navigable Rivers, Desert
