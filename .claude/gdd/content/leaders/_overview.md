## Purpose in the game

Leaders are ageless - persist across all three ages while civ changes around them. Each has an ability, agenda, 6-attribute specialization profile (Cultural/Diplomatic/Economic/Expansionist/Militaristic/Scientific), starting bias.

## Category-wide rules

- One leader per player, selected pre-game
- Persists all three ages unchanged
- Agenda defines opinion modifiers for diplomatic relations
- Leader specializes in 2 of 6 attributes (primary + secondary); gains attribute points via narrative events (1/age), Wonders, victories
- Personas (alt leader versions) may exist; pre-game-only selection

## VII-specific notes

- Leader-civ separation: leader and civ chosen independently (VI: one-to-one)
- Agenda-based diplomacy (replaces VI AI-personality-opaque system)
- Attribute tree is new meta-progression unlock target
- Persona system (alt leader variants) - VI had leader personas but not attribute specialization
- Leaders include non-monarchs (Franklin, Tubman, Lovelace) - historic-figure-who-mattered, not just heads of state
