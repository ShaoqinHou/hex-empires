## Identity

Tier 3 Exploration Age melee infantry. The Pikeman is the late medieval answer to heavy cavalry — long-pike infantry formations that could neutralize cavalry charges. While Civ VII removed the anti-cavalry class bonus as a system rule, the Pikeman is the apex Exploration Age ground infantry before the Modern Age.
