## Identity

Tier 1 Exploration Age naval unit. The Cog is the medieval cargo/warship — a single-masted sailing vessel. It represents the transition from oar-powered galleys to sail-powered ships. Available at Exploration Age start (or auto-upgrades from Quadrireme), it is the default naval unit for all civilizations entering the age.
