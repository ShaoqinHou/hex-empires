## Identity

Tier 2 Exploration Age siege unit. The Trebuchet is the heavy medieval counterweight siege machine — capable of hurling boulders over castle walls. It requires Metallurgy and upgrades the Catapult. The primary siege weapon for cracking defended cities in the middle Exploration Age, with significantly better bombard strength than the Catapult.
