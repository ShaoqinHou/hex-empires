## Identity

The Scout is the Antiquity Age exploration civilian unit. Scouts are fast, lightly armed units designed to explore the map, discover natural wonders, meet independent powers, and open ruins/goody huts. They can ignore difficult terrain movement penalties, making them the fastest land unit for map exploration despite their low combat defense.
