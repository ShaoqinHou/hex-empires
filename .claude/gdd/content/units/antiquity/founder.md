## Identity

The Founder is the unique starting civilian unit every civilization receives at the beginning of the Antiquity Age. It is functionally a special Settler whose sole purpose is to found the civilization's Capital City (a City, not a Town). The Founder cannot be trained after the Capital is founded — it is a one-time use unit provided at game start. It cannot be retrained once used.
