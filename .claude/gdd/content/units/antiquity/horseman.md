## Identity

Tier 3 Antiquity cavalry unit. The Horseman is the pinnacle cavalry unit of the Antiquity Age — a mounted warrior requiring Iron Working, the same technology that enables Phalanx. High movement (3) and solid combat strength (35) make it the fastest and most powerful Antiquity melee unit. Like all cavalry, it possesses the Swift ability (ZoC immunity).
