## Identity

Tier 1 Antiquity naval unit. The Galley is the first warship available, representing oar-driven vessels of the Bronze and early Iron Age Mediterranean. It requires Sailing and can operate on coastal waters and rivers. Its low CS makes it mainly useful for early coastal defense and transporting land units.
