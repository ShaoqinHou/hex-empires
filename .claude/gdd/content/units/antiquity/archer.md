## Identity

Tier 2 Antiquity ranged unit. Bow-armed infantry representing the classical Bronze Age archer corps. Upgraded from Slinger upon researching Bronze Working. Significantly stronger ranged attack than the Slinger; becomes the backbone of early Antiquity ranged defense.
