## Identity

Tier 2 Antiquity cavalry unit. The chariot represents Bronze Age wheeled warfare — fast, powerful, and immune to Zone of Control. Requiring The Wheel technology, it unlocks the cavalry class for most civilizations in mid-Antiquity. Its high movement (3) and ZoC immunity make it excellent for flanking maneuvers.
