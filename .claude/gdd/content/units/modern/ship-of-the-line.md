## Identity

Tier 1 Modern Age heavy naval unit. The Ship of the Line is the 18th-century sailing warship of the line-of-battle — the predecessor to the Ironclad and the transitional naval unit entering the Modern Age. Available without prerequisites at Modern Age start, representing the upgrade of Exploration Age Galleons.
