## Identity

Tier 1 Modern Age light naval unit. The Ironclad is the first steam-powered warship — an armored, steam-driven vessel representing the industrial naval revolution of the mid-19th century. Requires Steam Engine. Faster than Exploration Age sail ships (movement 4) and significantly more powerful.
