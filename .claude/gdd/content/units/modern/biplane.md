## Identity

Tier 1 Modern Age fighter aircraft. The Biplane is the earliest military aircraft — WWI-era open-cockpit fighters. It requires Flight and can intercept enemy air units. With movement 8 and the ability to cover large map areas, air units represent a new dimension of warfare in the Modern Age.
