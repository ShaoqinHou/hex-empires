## Identity

Tier 1 Modern Age melee infantry. Line Infantry represents the musket-armed infantry formations of the 17th–18th century — disciplined, volley-fire battalions. Available at Modern Age start without a technology prerequisite, it is the default infantry for civilizations without a unique Modern Tier 1 infantry replacement.
