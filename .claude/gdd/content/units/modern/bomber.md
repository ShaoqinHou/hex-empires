## Identity

Tier 1 Modern Age bomber aircraft. The Bomber is the WWI–WWII era strategic/tactical bombing platform. It has very high bombard strength (65) making it the most powerful anti-fortification and anti-district weapon in the game. Requires Flight. Slow for an air unit (movement 10) but capable of reaching distant targets.
