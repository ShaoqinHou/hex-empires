## Identity

Tier 2 Modern Age ranged unit. The Field Gun represents the rifled breech-loading artillery of the 19th century — significantly more powerful and accurate than smooth-bore field cannon. Requires Industrialization; the ranged counterpart to Rifle Infantry in the mid-Modern lineup.
