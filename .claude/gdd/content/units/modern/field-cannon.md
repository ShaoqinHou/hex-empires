## Identity

Tier 1 Modern Age ranged unit. The Field Cannon is the smooth-bore bronze cannon of the 17th–18th century — Napoleon's "beautiful daughters." Available at Modern Age start, it transitions from the Exploration Age's gunpowder Arquebusier to dedicated artillery. High ranged strength (45) makes it dangerous against open infantry.
