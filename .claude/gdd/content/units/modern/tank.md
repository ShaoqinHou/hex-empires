## Identity

Tier 3 Modern Age cavalry unit. The Tank is the definitive 20th-century armored cavalry — fast, powerful, and immune to Zone of Control. Requiring both Metal Casting and Armor, it is the most powerful land combat unit in the base game. Movement 4 makes it the fastest armored unit in the game.
