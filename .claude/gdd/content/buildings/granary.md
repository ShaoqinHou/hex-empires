## Identity

The Granary is the foundational food Warehouse building of the Antiquity Age. Representing organized grain storage, it is one of the oldest civic infrastructure types in human history. As a Warehouse building it is **Ageless** — it persists with full effects through all three age transitions and cannot be overbuilt.

- Historical period: Neolithic surplus storage through ancient civilizations
- Flavor: The first step from subsistence farming to surplus-driven urban growth

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 55 Production
- Maintenance: 0 Gold (Warehouse — no upkeep)
- Yield(s): +1 Food (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Food on each Farm, Pasture, and Plantation improvement within the settlement
- Requires: Agriculture (Technology)
- Slot: Warehouse (Ageless; purchasable in Towns as well as Cities)
