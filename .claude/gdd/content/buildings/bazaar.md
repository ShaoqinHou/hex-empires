## Identity

The Bazaar is the entry-level Exploration Age gold building — the covered marketplace of the Islamic and Silk Road trading world. It represents the transition from open-air ancient markets to the organized commercial infrastructure of medieval trade. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Islamic bazaars from Baghdad to Constantinople, Silk Road trading posts
- Flavor: Silk, spice, and the mathematics of profit in a thousand languages
