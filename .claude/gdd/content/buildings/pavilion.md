## Identity

The Pavilion is the high-tier Exploration Age culture building — an ornate open-air structure representing the public spaces and ceremonial architecture of medieval and early-modern societies. It provides a bonus to Quarters formed in its tile. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Chinese imperial pavilions, Ottoman kiosks, Mughal garden pavilions
- Flavor: A building that exists to be beautiful — culture as its own justification

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 250 Production
- Maintenance: 3 Happiness, 3 Gold
- Yield(s): +5 Culture (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Culture per adjacent Mountain, Natural Wonder, or Wonder
- Requires: Architecture (Technology)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless)
