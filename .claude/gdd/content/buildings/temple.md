## Identity

The Temple is the Exploration Age happiness/faith building — the evolution from the Antiquity Altar to a more formal religious institution. It represents the great cathedral-building age of the medieval world, from European cathedrals to Islamic mosques to Hindu and Buddhist temple complexes. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Gothic cathedrals, Islamic mosques (8th–15th centuries), Hindu temple cities
- Flavor: Spires and minarets — the city's skyline as a statement of belief

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 200 Production
