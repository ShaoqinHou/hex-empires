## Identity

The Garden is an Antiquity Age food building representing urban green space and managed water features — the pleasure gardens, irrigation canals, and parks of ancient cities from Babylon to Rome. It provides both food and a settlement-limit bonus, rewarding founders of new cities. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Ancient Mesopotamia, Persian gardens, Hellenistic civic parks
- Flavor: "The Hanging Gardens were a lie. Every garden is its own small wonder."

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 90 Production
- Maintenance: 2 Gold
- Yield(s): +3 Food (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Food per adjacent Coastal, Lake, River, or Wonder tile
- Requires: Irrigation (Technology)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless)
