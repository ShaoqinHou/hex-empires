## Identity

The Bath is an Antiquity Age food building representing public bathing infrastructure — Roman thermae, Greek balaneion, and similar sanitation facilities. It provides the highest base Food yield of any Antiquity standard building and boosts growth rate, but has a placement requirement: must be on a River tile. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Roman Empire and Greek city-states, also attested in Indus Valley
- Flavor: Aqueducts, heated pools, and the Roman genius for turning water into civilization

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 130 Production
- Maintenance: 2 Happiness, 2 Gold
- Yield(s): +4 Food (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Food per adjacent Coastal, Lake, River, or Wonder tile
- Requires: Currency (Technology)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless); must be placed on a River-adjacent tile
