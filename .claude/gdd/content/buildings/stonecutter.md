## Identity

The Stonecutter is an Exploration Age Warehouse production building specializing in mineral extraction — mines and quarries. It represents the guild-organized stone masonry and metal extraction industries of the medieval world. Ageless.

- Historical period: Medieval European quarrying guilds, Islamic stone-cutting traditions
- Flavor: Hammer and chisel on stone — the patient work of cities that plan to last
