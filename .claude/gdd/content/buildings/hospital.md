## Identity

The Hospital is the high-tier Exploration Age food and growth building — representing the Islamic bimaristan, the medieval European hospital, and the growing institutionalization of healing. It provides both high Food yield and a growth rate bonus. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Islamic bimaristan (9th century onward), medieval European infirmaries
- Flavor: The physician's art scaled to the city — where plague ends and population begins again

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 250 Production
- Maintenance: 3 Happiness, 3 Gold
- Yield(s): +5 Food (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Food per adjacent Coastal, River, or Wonder tile
- Requires: Urban Planning (Civic)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless)
