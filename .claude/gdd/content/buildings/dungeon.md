## Identity

The Dungeon is an Exploration Age military/production building — representing the castle infrastructure and fortification works of the medieval period. Despite the name, it functions as a production building linked to the Castles tech and medieval defensive infrastructure. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Medieval castle dungeons as fortified keeps (not primarily prisons until later)
- Flavor: Stone towers and iron-banded gates — the castle's heart was always the dungeon
