## Identity

The Port is the entry-level Modern Age gold building for coastal cities — the modern commercial harbor and naval yard. It represents the steam-powered port infrastructure of the Industrial Age from Liverpool to Singapore. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Liverpool Docks (1840s), Hamburg harbor, Singapore colonial port
- Flavor: The world made liquid — goods, troops, and empires moving by sea

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 550 Production
- Maintenance: 4 Happiness
- Yield(s): +5 Gold (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Gold per adjacent Coastal, River, or Wonder tile
- Requires: Steam Engine (Technology)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless)
