## Identity

The Amphitheater is the high-tier Antiquity Age culture building — an open-air performance space for drama, poetry, and civic spectacle. It provides the highest Culture yield of any Antiquity standard building and uniquely boosts Wonder construction speed, making it valuable for wonder-racing strategies. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Greek theater tradition, Roman amphitheaters
- Flavor: The semicircle of stone where tragedy and comedy first distinguished themselves

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 195 Production
- Maintenance: 2 Happiness, 2 Gold
- Yield(s): +4 Culture (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Culture per adjacent Mountain, Natural Wonder, or Wonder
- Requires: Engineering (Technology)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless)
