## Identity

The Shipyard is the Exploration Age naval production building — the dry dock and boatyard of the Age of Sail. It represents the Venetian Arsenal, the Dutch shipbuilding industry, and the Portuguese carrack yards that drove exploration. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Venetian Arsenal (13th century onward), Portuguese Age of Discovery shipyards
- Flavor: The smell of fresh timber and pitch — where continents become reachable

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 200 Production
