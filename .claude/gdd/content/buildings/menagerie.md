## Identity

The Menagerie is the high-tier Exploration Age happiness building — a collection of exotic animals kept for display, representing the Age of Exploration's fascination with the natural world and the exotic. Royal menageries were status symbols for European and Asian monarchs. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Tower of London menagerie (13th century), Aztec royal zoo, Mughal gardens
- Flavor: The lion in the city garden — proof that the world comes to you
