## Identity

The Schoolhouse is the entry-level Modern Age science building — the mandatory public school of the 19th century, institutionalizing mass education for the first time. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Prussian public school system (early 19th century), British Elementary Education Act (1870)
- Flavor: The teacher with thirty students — the first time a government decided everyone should read
