## Identity

The Rail Station is the Modern Age gold infrastructure building — the railway station that connected industrial cities and enabled the mass movement of goods and people. From Grand Central to Gare du Nord, rail stations became the symbolic heart of industrial cities. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Liverpool and Manchester Railway (1830), transcontinental railroads (1860s–1880s)
- Flavor: The timetable is civilization — the train departs at 9:15 and the empire depends on it
