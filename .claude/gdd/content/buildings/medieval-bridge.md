## Identity

The Medieval Bridge is the Exploration Age iteration of the bridge infrastructure series. Like the Ancient Bridge it is **repeatable** — can be built multiple times in a settlement. Represents the stone arch bridges of medieval Europe and the Islamic world. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Medieval stone bridges (Pont d'Avignon, medieval London Bridge)
- Flavor: Toll-booth and lifeline — every medieval bridge was both
