## Identity

The Academy is the high-tier Antiquity Age science building — the philosophical school, the Platonic Academy, the Lyceum. It provides the highest Science yield of any Antiquity standard building and the most Codex slots, making it the cornerstone of science-focused cities. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Plato's Academy (~387 BCE), Aristotle's Lyceum, great Hellenistic schools
- Flavor: "Let no one ignorant of geometry enter here." — inscription at Plato's Academy

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 195 Production
- Maintenance: 2 Happiness, 2 Gold
- Yield(s): +4 Science (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Science per adjacent Resource or Wonder tile
- Requires: Mathematics (Technology)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless); has 3 Codex slots
