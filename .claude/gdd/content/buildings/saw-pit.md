## Identity

The Saw Pit is an Antiquity Age production Warehouse building focused on forestry and camp-based industry. A saw pit was a trench over which timber was placed for hand-sawing into planks — one of the most ubiquitous pre-industrial woodworking installations. Ageless — persists through all age transitions.

- Historical period: Ancient through early-modern timber trade
- Flavor: The rhythm of the two-man saw, turning raw forest into building material
