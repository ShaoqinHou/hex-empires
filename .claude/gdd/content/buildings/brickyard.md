## Identity

The Brickyard is an Antiquity Age production Warehouse building that boosts extraction industries: mines, quarries, and clay pits. Brick-making is among the oldest forms of manufactured building material, attested in Mesopotamia and the Indus Valley as far back as 7500 BCE. Ageless — persists through all age transitions.

- Historical period: Mesopotamia, Indus Valley, ancient Egypt
- Flavor: Kilns, clay, and the foundation of every city wall and palace

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 55 Production
- Maintenance: 0 Gold (Warehouse — no upkeep)
- Yield(s): +1 Production (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Production on each Clay Pit, Mine, and Quarry improvement within the settlement
- Requires: Pottery (Technology)
- Slot: Warehouse (Ageless; purchasable in Towns)
