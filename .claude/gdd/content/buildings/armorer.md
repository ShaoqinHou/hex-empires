## Identity

The Armorer (listed as "Armorer" in the production buildings table) is the entry-level Exploration Age military production building — succeeding the Barracks. It represents the skilled metalworking guilds and royal armories of the medieval period that equipped professional armies with plate armor and siege equipment. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Medieval European armories, Islamic metalworking tradition
- Flavor: The armorer's guild — where steel becomes soldiers
