## Identity

The Lighthouse is an Antiquity Age gold building built by coastal and maritime-focused cities. As a navigational aid, it represents ancient Rome's Pharos-inspired harbor infrastructure and Greek naval tradition. Non-ageless — superseded by Exploration Age Port.

- Historical period: Lighthouse of Alexandria (3rd century BCE), Colossus of Rhodes era
- Flavor: Fire on a tower, guiding merchants home through dark water
