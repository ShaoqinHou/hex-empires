## Identity

The Museum is the entry-level Modern Age culture building — the institutional home for artifacts, natural history collections, and cultural heritage. It represents the great 19th-century national museums from the British Museum to the Smithsonian. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: 19th century — British Museum, Louvre, Smithsonian
- Flavor: The Empire brought home to the metropolis — culture made permanent
