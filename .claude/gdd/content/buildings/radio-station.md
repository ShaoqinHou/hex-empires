## Identity

The Radio Station is the high-tier Modern Age happiness building — representing mass media and broadcast culture of the early 20th century. Radio became the first technology to deliver entertainment and propaganda simultaneously to millions. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: BBC (1922), NBC (1926), golden age of radio (1930s–1950s)
- Flavor: The voice in every home — happiness as information, and information as control
