## Identity

The Military Academy is the entry-level Modern Age military production building — institutionalizing military education from West Point (1802) to Saint-Cyr. It replaces informal barracks training with systematic officer education. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: West Point (1802), École Spéciale Militaire de Saint-Cyr (1802), Sandhurst (1812)
- Flavor: The officer class — war made professional, then made a profession
