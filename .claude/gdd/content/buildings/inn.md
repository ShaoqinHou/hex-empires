## Identity

The Inn is an Exploration Age food building representing the hospitality infrastructure that supported the growing overland and maritime trade networks of the medieval world — caravanserais, pilgrim hostels, and roadside taverns. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Medieval caravanserai (Islamic world), European roadside inns, pilgrimage lodges
- Flavor: Every trade route runs on the promise of a warm bed and a meal at the end

## Stats / numeric attributes
