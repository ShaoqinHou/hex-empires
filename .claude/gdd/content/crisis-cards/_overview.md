## Purpose in the game

End-of-age narrative disruption events. Trigger at ~85-90% age progress in Antiquity and Exploration. Player chooses Crisis Policies to resolve. Must resolve before age transition can proceed.

## Category-wide rules

- Trigger stage 1 at ~85% age progress; stage 2 at ~88%; stage 3 at ~90%
- Player Crisis Policies unlock: 2 at Stage 1, 3 at Stage 2, 4 at Stage 3
- Crisis must resolve before age transition fires
- Crisis themed to age (plague Antiquity, revolt/revolution Exploration)
- Specific choices may unlock crisis-locked governments (Revolutions -> Revolutionary Republic etc.)
