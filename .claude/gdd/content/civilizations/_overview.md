## Purpose in the game

Civs are the age-gated nation identities. Player picks one per age at age transition (capital stays; civ changes). Each civ has unique bonus, unique unit, unique civic, unique improvement, unique quarter pair (2 unique buildings).

## Category-wide rules

- Per-age civ rosters (10 Antiquity + 10 Exploration + 10 Modern base; +DLC)
- Civ swap at every age transition
- Historical/geographical unlock paths (e.g. Egypt -> Abbasid)
- Each civ unique unit+civic+improvement+2-building-quarter
- Civ uniques are Ageless - survive transition when other civ uniques replaced

## VII-specific notes

- Per-age civ swap is a defining VII mechanic - VI had one civ whole game
- Historical-path unlocks (Egypt->Abbasid, Maurya->Chola) add narrative coherence
- Geographical/resource unlocks (horses -> Mongolia) reward strategic positioning
- Capital persists; civilization identity changes around it
