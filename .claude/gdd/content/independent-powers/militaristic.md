## Identity

Militaristic Independent Powers represent frontier warrior cultures and martial confederacies. They align with the military victory path. Dispersing one yields 125 Production.

- Historical theme: frontier encampments, raider confederacies, mercenary states
- Icon: sword/crossed-blades; red color coding in the UI

## Stats / numeric attributes

- **Disperse yield:** 125 Production [source: Fandom WebSearch summary]
- **Default attitude:** hostile (most common); neutral possible
- **Hostile behavior:** sends military units toward nearest player territory every few turns
