## Identity

Etelkoz is the Magyar tribal homeland — a steppe region in modern Ukraine/Moldova where the Magyar confederacy gathered before their migration west into the Carpathian Basin in the late 9th century CE.

- Historical period: ~830-895 CE; Magyar confederation prior to Hungarian Kingdom founding
- Cultural identity: Magyar (Ugric/Turkic steppe nomad heritage)
- Flavor: nomadic cavalry confederation; later becomes Kingdom of Hungary as a full civilization
