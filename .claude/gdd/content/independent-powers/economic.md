## Identity

Economic Independent Powers represent trading hubs, merchant republics, and resource-rich entrepots. They align with the economic victory path. Dispersing one yields 125 Gold.

- Historical theme: market cities, caravan crossroads, port trading towns
- Icon: coin or merchant symbol; gold/yellow color coding in the UI
