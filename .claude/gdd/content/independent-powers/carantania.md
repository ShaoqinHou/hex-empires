## Identity

Carantania was an early Slavic principality in the Eastern Alps (roughly modern-day Austria/Slovenia), existing from approximately the 7th to 9th centuries CE. It is notable as one of the first documented Slavic states.

- Historical period: ~7th-9th century CE; earliest Slavic principality in the Alps
- Cultural identity: Slav
- Flavor: mountain principality; proto-state that later merged into Frankish and then Bavarian spheres
