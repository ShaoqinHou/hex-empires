## Identity

The Cham people established a series of Hindu/Buddhist kingdoms (Champa) along the central coast of present-day Vietnam from approximately the 2nd to 17th centuries CE. Champa was a seafaring and trading civilization with strong Indianized cultural influence.

- Historical period: ~2nd-17th century CE; Champa kingdoms
- Cultural identity: Cham (Southeast Asian Indianized)
- Flavor: seafaring maritime traders; Hindu/Buddhist temple builders
