## Identity

Taiga — the "Tundra Vegetated" biome modifier. Boreal conifer forest at high latitudes. The cultural analog to the tropical biome's scientific Rainforest. Represents the vast northern forest belt of Siberia, Canada, and Scandinavia. Unique Culture yield reflects the deep cultural traditions of northern indigenous peoples.

- Historical period: Siberian taiga, Canadian boreal forest, Scandinavian pine forests
- Flavor: Ancient northern forest; Production and Culture in isolation.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +2 Production, +1 Culture
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (vegetated modifier)
- Defense modifier: +2 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Dense pine forest provides +2 CS defensive cover
- Can build: Woodcutter/Lumber Mill (auto-improvement), Roads
