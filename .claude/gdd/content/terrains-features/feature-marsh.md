## Identity

Marsh — the "Grassland Wet" biome modifier. Waterlogged grassland with standing water and reeds. Found in temperate lowland areas near rivers and coasts. In Civ VII the marsh is the wet variant of the Grassland biome, providing high combined yields at the cost of difficult movement.

- Historical period: Temperate wetlands; Mesopotamian marshes, English fens, Everglades
- Flavor: Fertile but inaccessible; the food-production wet tile.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +2 Food, +2 Production
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (wet modifier)
- Defense modifier: 0 [INFERRED]
- Combat rules: Movement-stopping; no combat advantage
- Can build: Limited improvements (wet-compatible); potential fresh water settlement bonus
