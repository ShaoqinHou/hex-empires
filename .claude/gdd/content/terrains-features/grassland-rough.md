## Identity

Hilly grassland — rolling hills covered in grasses. Balances Food and Production, trading some of the flat biome food surplus for defensible high ground and mining potential. Represents temperate foothills where pastoral and early industrial activities coexist.

- Historical period: Temperate hill country; pastoral and early mining zones
- Flavor: Defensive high ground with mixed food/production output.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +1 Food, +2 Production
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (rough modifier)
- Defense modifier: +3 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Defender gains +3 CS on rough terrain; attacker expends all MP entering
- Can build: Mine (auto-improvement on hills), Quarry where stone resources present, Roads
