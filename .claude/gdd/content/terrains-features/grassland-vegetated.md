## Identity

Forested grassland — temperate woodland covering grassy terrain. Feature name is "Forest" (also "Woods"). Classic temperate deciduous and mixed forest belt found in northern Europe, eastern North America, and northern Asia. Same numeric yield as Grassland Rough but via vegetation rather than elevation.

- Historical period: Temperate deciduous and mixed woodland zones
- Flavor: Dense woodland providing balanced food/production with defensive forest cover.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +1 Food, +2 Production
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (vegetated modifier)
- Defense modifier: +2 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Forest cover grants +2 CS to defender; all movement points depleted on entry
- Can build: Woodcutter/Lumber Mill (auto-improvement), Roads reduce crossing to 1 MP
