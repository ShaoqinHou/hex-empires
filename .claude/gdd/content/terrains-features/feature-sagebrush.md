## Identity

Sagebrush Steppe — the "Desert Vegetated" biome modifier. Drought-resistant shrubs on arid terrain. Unique in being the only non-aquatic vegetated terrain with a Gold yield component. Represents the sagebrush ecosystems of the American Great Basin, Iranian plateau, and North African desert margins.

- Historical period: American Great Basin, Central Asian steppe, North African scrubland
- Flavor: Scrubby desert vegetation with trade value; the gold-yielding land tile.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +2 Production, +1 Gold
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (vegetated modifier)
- Defense modifier: +2 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Sparse scrub provides +2 CS defensive cover
- Can build: Woodcutter (limited), Roads
