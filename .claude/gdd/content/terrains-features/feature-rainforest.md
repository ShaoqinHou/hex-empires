## Identity

Rainforest — the "Tropical Vegetated" biome modifier. Dense equatorial jungle producing Science as its unique yield. A VII innovation: previous Civ games modeled jungle as a penalty tile requiring clearing. VII makes Rainforest a productive scientific tile, reflecting biodiversity research value and indigenous knowledge systems.

- Historical period: Amazon, Congo, and Southeast Asian rainforest belts
- Flavor: Impenetrable jungle with hidden scientific riches.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +2 Production, +1 Science
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (vegetated modifier)
- Defense modifier: +2 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Dense jungle provides +2 CS cover; movement depleted on entry
- Can build: Woodcutter (limited), Plantation where luxury resources present, Roads
