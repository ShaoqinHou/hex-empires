## Identity

Tundra hills — elevated arctic terrain with rocky outcrops and permafrost. Represents subarctic mountain foothills and highland moors. Pure Production tile — the cold hills yield minerals and stone but little food. The tundra hills belt is historically associated with reindeer herding and mineral extraction.

- Historical period: Subarctic highlands, Ural and Scandinavian foothills, Alaskan interior ranges
- Flavor: Cold rocky heights; pure production, no food.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +3 Production
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (rough modifier)
- Defense modifier: +3 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Defender gains +3 CS; movement depleted on entry
- Can build: Mine (auto-improvement), Quarry where stone present, Roads
