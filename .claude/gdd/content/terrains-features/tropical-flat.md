## Identity

Flat tropical terrain — open equatorial lowland with high humidity but no vegetation cover. A VII-new biome, separated from Grassland to model distinct equatorial ecosystems. Represents the flat coastal plains, river valleys, and cleared lowlands of tropical latitudes. Highest Food output matching Grassland Flat.

- Historical period: Equatorial lowlands; Mesoamerican coasts, West African plains, Southeast Asian river valleys
- Flavor: Hot, humid open plain; food-rich equatorial terrain.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +3 Food
- Movement cost: 1 MP (flat modifier)
- Defense modifier: 0
- Combat rules: No special rules; standard unit entry
- Can build: Farm, Plantation (where resources), Quarters, Roads
