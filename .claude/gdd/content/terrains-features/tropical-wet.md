## Identity

Mangrove wetland — coastal tropical wetland with flooded root systems. Feature name is "Mangrove." Found along tropical coastlines worldwide, mangroves are uniquely productive ecosystems that combine aquatic and terrestrial yields. In Civ VII this is modeled as the only wet terrain yielding Science (matching Rainforest biome).

- Historical period: Tropical coastal wetlands globally; Southeast Asia, West Africa, Caribbean
- Flavor: Coastal jungle swamp; Food and Production with unique Science component.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +1 Food, +2 Production, +1 Science
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (wet modifier)
- Defense modifier: 0 [INFERRED]
- Combat rules: Movement-stopping entry; no confirmed defense bonus
- Can build: Limited to mangrove/coastal-compatible improvements
