## Identity

Tropical rainforest — dense equatorial jungle. Feature name is "Rainforest." The VII incarnation gives Rainforest a unique Science yield, reflecting biodiversity research value absent in earlier games. Represents the Amazon, Congo Basin, and Southeast Asian rainforest belts. The most scientifically valuable non-campus terrain.

- Historical period: Amazon Basin, Congo rainforest, Southeast Asian tropical forests
- Flavor: Dense equatorial jungle with unique scientific value.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +2 Production, +1 Science
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (vegetated modifier)
- Defense modifier: +2 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Dense jungle provides +2 CS defensive cover; movement depleted on entry
- Can build: Woodcutter (limited), Plantation (where resources), Roads
