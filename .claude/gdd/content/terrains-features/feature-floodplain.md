## Identity

Floodplain — tiles adjacent to or on navigable rivers that are vulnerable to periodic flooding. Represents the fertile river floodplains of history: the Nile Delta, Mesopotamian alluvial plains, and Yellow River basin. Flooding damages buildings but historically fertilizes the soil, potentially granting yield bonuses post-flood.

- Historical period: Nile, Tigris-Euphrates, Indus, and Yellow River floodplains
- Flavor: Rich but risky terrain; flooding damages settlements but enriches tiles.
