## Identity

Wet grassland — marshy terrain combining high food output with waterlogged ground. Feature name is "Marsh." Found in river deltas, coastal lowlands, and flood-prone temperate regions. Highest combined Food+Production in the Grassland biome. Slow to traverse, no combat advantage.

- Historical period: Temperate wetlands, river deltas, coastal lowlands
- Flavor: Fertile but sluggish terrain; maximum yields at the cost of mobility.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +2 Food, +2 Production
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (wet modifier)
- Defense modifier: 0 [INFERRED]
- Combat rules: Movement-stopping on entry; no confirmed defense bonus for wet modifier
- Can build: Auto-improvement limited in marsh (wet-compatible improvements only)
