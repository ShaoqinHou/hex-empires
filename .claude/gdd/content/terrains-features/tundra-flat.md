## Identity

Flat tundra — open arctic or sub-arctic terrain with permafrost and low-growing vegetation. Snow is not a separate biome in VII — it is subsumed visually into Tundra. Surprisingly high Food yield (3F matching Grassland Flat) represents the seasonal abundance of tundra ecosystems, including hunting, fishing, and gathering.

- Historical period: Arctic and subarctic flatlands; Siberian tundra, Canadian arctic, Scandinavian fells
- Flavor: Bleak but food-rich open tundra; the northern frontier.
