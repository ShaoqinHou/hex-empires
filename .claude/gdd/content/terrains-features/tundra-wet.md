## Identity

Tundra bog — waterlogged peat bog in arctic or subarctic terrain. Feature name is "Bog." Represents the permafrost-based peat bogs of Siberia, northern Canada, and Scandinavia. Uniquely yields Food, Production, and Culture — the richest variety in the Tundra biome, combining subsistence hunting (Food), peat extraction (Production), and cultural heritage (Culture).

- Historical period: Siberian peat bogs, Scandinavian mires, Canadian muskeg
- Flavor: Ancient waterlogged peat land; diverse yields in a harsh environment.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +1 Food, +2 Production, +1 Culture
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (wet modifier)
- Defense modifier: 0 [INFERRED]
- Combat rules: Movement-stopping entry; no confirmed defense bonus
- Can build: Limited to wet/bog-compatible improvements
