## Identity

Desert scrubland — sparse drought-resistant shrubs on arid terrain. Feature name is "Sagebrush Steppe." Represents the sagebrush ecosystems of the American Great Basin, Central Asian scrublands, and North African desert margins. Unique in being the only non-water vegetated terrain with a Gold yield component.

- Historical period: American Great Basin, Central Asian arid scrubland, North African desert margins
- Flavor: Desert scrub with trade-route value; the gold-yielding vegetation tile.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +2 Production, +1 Gold
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (vegetated modifier)
- Defense modifier: +2 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Scrub provides +2 CS defensive cover
- Can build: Woodcutter (limited), auto-improvement for scrubland, Roads
