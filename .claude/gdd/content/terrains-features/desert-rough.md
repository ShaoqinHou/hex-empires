## Identity

Desert hills — rocky elevated desert terrain. Represents the rocky escarpments, mesas, and buttes of arid regions. Yields pure Production with no Food offset — excellent for manufacturing cities in dry climates but requires food support from elsewhere.

- Historical period: Arid rocky uplands; Arabian plateau, Atacama escarpments
- Flavor: Rocky desert heights; maximum production, zero food.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +3 Production
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (rough modifier)
- Defense modifier: +3 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Defender gains +3 CS; movement depleted on entry
- Can build: Mine (auto-improvement), Quarry where stone resources present, Roads
