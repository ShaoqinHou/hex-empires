# Emerging Traps

Staging area for traps promoted from `issues.md` PRINCIPLE-GAP entries.

**Workflow (manual, no automation):**

1. The commit-review orchestrator logs novel finding shapes as `[PRINCIPLE-GAP]`
   entries in `.codex/workflow/issues.md`.
2. Periodically (quarterly is plenty), scan `issues.md` for PRINCIPLE-GAP
   patterns. When the same shape appears ≥4 times, add a row below with a
   draft description.
3. After a human reviews and confirms, move the trap to the relevant
   `.codex/rules/` document and delete the row here.

Automation of step 2 is out of scope — it would add complexity for a
low-frequency manual pass.
