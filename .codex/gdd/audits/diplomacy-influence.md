### F-01: Influence as 6th yield -- MATCH

**Location:** `packages/engine/src/types/Yields.ts:1`, `packages/engine/src/types/GameState.ts:164`  
**GDD reference:** section "Influence as Diplomatic Yield"  
**Severity:** N/A (compliant)  
**VII says:** Influence is the sixth major yield (Food/Production/Science/Culture/Gold/Influence). Lives on PlayerState as a stockpile; accumulated per-turn.  
**Engine does:** `YieldType` union includes `influence`. `PlayerState.influence: number` exists. `ResourceChangeCalculator.influencePerTurn` calculated and applied in `resourceSystem.ts`. `Governor.yieldType` can be `influence`.  
**Assessment:** Fully compliant. Influence is modeled as a first-class yield. No gap.

---

### F-02: Influence generation sources -- DIVERGED

**Location:** `packages/engine/src/state/ResourceChangeCalculator.ts:154-160`  
**GDD reference:** section "Influence as Diplomatic Yield -- Key generators by age"  
**Severity:** HIGH  
**Effort:** M (1-3 days)  
**VII says:** Influence generated by buildings (Monument +1, Garden +1, Guildhall +2, Radio Station +4, etc.), social policies (Oratory +2), Diplomatic Attribute Tree (+1/Age on Palace), Wonders (Weiyang Palace +6, Serpent Mound +4), leaders (Machiavelli +3/Age), Town Hub (+2/connected settlement). Base ~10/turn. No alliance-per-turn bonus in VII sources.  
**Engine does:** `influencePerTurn = cityCount + (alliances * 2)`. That is +1 per city, +2 per alliance. No building contributions, no policy contributions, no wonder contributions, no leader ability contributions. The alliance bonus is not a VII-documented generator.  
**Gap:** The entire content layer of influence generation (buildings, wonders, policies, leader abilities) is absent. Base rate should be ~10/turn from buildings, not 1 per city.  
**Recommendation:** Wire influence yield from building MODIFY_YIELD effects (already supported by EffectDef). Add Diplomatic Attribute Tree and policy MODIFY_YIELD effects. Remove the ad-hoc alliance bonus from ResourceChangeCalculator and model it as a proper wonder/attribute effect.

### F-03: Denounce -- MISMATCH (wrong pathway, wrong opinion delta, no Influence cost)

**Location:** `packages/engine/src/systems/diplomacySystem.ts:224-235`  
**GDD reference:** section "Sanctions -- Denounce"  
**Severity:** MED  
**Effort:** S (half-day)  
**VII says:** Denounce is a Sanction that applies -60 opinion immediately. It is an Influence-spend action routed through the Sanction pathway.  
**Engine does:** Denounce lives in the `PROPOSE_DIPLOMACY` switch case (not DIPLOMATIC_SANCTION) and costs 0 Influence. Delta is relationship - 25 (not -60). The `hasDenounced` flag is not a VII entity.  
**Gap:** (1) Denounce should cost Influence and flow through DIPLOMATIC_SANCTION. (2) Delta is -25 vs GDD -60. Denounce is supposed to be the most severe sanction.  
**Recommendation:** Move Denounce into DIPLOMATIC_SANCTION handler. Add Influence cost. Change delta to -60.

### F-04: Endeavor Support/Accept/Reject 3-way response -- MISSING

**Location:** `packages/engine/src/systems/diplomacySystem.ts:304-350`  
**GDD reference:** section "Endeavors"  
**Severity:** HIGH  
**Effort:** M (1-3 days)  
**VII says:** Endeavor response has three options: SUPPORT (bilateral full bonus), ACCEPT (free for recipient, reduced bonus for recipient), REJECT (proposer refunded, recipient pays rejection cost). The three-way choice drives the Endorse feedback loop.  
**Engine does:** `handleEndeavor` fires immediately on DIPLOMATIC_ENDEAVOR action. The endeavor is placed in `activeEndeavors` instantly without the target player being asked. No `RESPOND_ENDEAVOR` action exists in GameAction union.  
**Gap:** The entire bilateral negotiation mechanic is absent. Endeavors are unilateral impositions rather than negotiated agreements.  
**Recommendation:** Add `RESPOND_ENDEAVOR` to GameAction union with `{ endeavorId, response: support|accept|reject }`. Change handleEndeavor to create a pending endeavor offer. Add RESPOND_ENDEAVOR handler applying the three outcome branches.

### F-05: Espionage system -- MISSING

**Location:** Entire engine  
**GDD reference:** section "Espionage (No Spy Units!)"  
**Severity:** HIGH  
**Effort:** L (week+)  
**VII says:** Espionage is a core diplomacy channel. No spy units. Actions are abstract Influence-funded covert ops: initial cost + per-turn drain + completion with detection roll. 8 age-specific actions (Steal Gov Secrets, Steal Tech Research, Infiltrate Military, Open the Gates, Sabotage Specialist Morale, Infiltrate Positions, Spread Fake Artifacts, Sabotage Space Program). Counterespionage available. Espionage cancelled if Influence hits 0. Modern espionage attacks Legacy progress directly.  
**Engine does:** No INITIATE_ESPIONAGE action. No `activeEspionageActions` on PlayerState. No detection roll logic. No Counterespionage. No per-turn Influence drain for covert ops. No espionage system file.  
**Gap:** The espionage channel is completely absent. This is one of the three major spend-action categories alongside Endeavors and Sanctions.  
**Recommendation:** Add `PlayerState.activeEspionageActions` (optional, per M12 pattern). Add `INITIATE_ESPIONAGE` and `COUNTERESPIONAGE` to GameAction. Create `espionageSystem.ts`. Wire into GameEngine pipeline. Add Influence=0 cancellation to END_TURN path.

### F-06: Treaties (Open Borders, Trade Relations) -- MISSING

**Location:** Entire engine  
**GDD reference:** section "Treaties"  
**Severity:** MED  
**Effort:** M (1-3 days)  
**VII says:** Treaties: Open Borders (15t, territory passable), Improve Trade Relations (+1 Trade Route slot with partner), Denounce Military Presence (border opens, 15t). Proposer spends Influence; bilateral on acceptance.  
**Engine does:** DiplomacyRelation has no treaty fields. No `PROPOSE_TREATY` action. No `activeTreaties` array. The `hasFriendship` boolean partially substitutes but is not an Influence-spend timed agreement.  
**Gap:** The entire treaty category is unimplemented. Open Borders has direct gameplay impact (blocking unit movement across borders).  
**Recommendation:** Add `activeTreaties: ReadonlyArray<DiplomaticTreaty>` to DiplomacyRelation. Add `PROPOSE_TREATY` to GameAction. Handle cost + bilateral effect + timed expiry in updateDiplomacyCounters.

### F-07: Independent Powers interaction -- MISSING

### F-08: War Support -- CLOSE (combat cap wrong, happiness formula wrong, no purchase action)

**Location:** `packages/engine/src/systems/combatSystem.ts:211-232`, `packages/engine/src/state/HappinessUtils.ts:76-87`  
**GDD reference:** sections "War Support" and "War Weariness"  
**Severity:** MED  
**Effort:** S (half-day)  
**VII says:** Combat penalty: -1 CS per negative war support point, max -20. Happiness: -3 founded / -5 pre-war acquired / -7 captured during war (per settlement acquisition type). War Support purchasable for 180 Influence.  
**Engine does:** Combat penalty: -1 CS per negative point, cap -10 (half of GDD). Happiness: threshold-based (-3 if warSupport < -30, -5 if warSupport < 0) ignoring acquisition type. No ADD_WAR_SUPPORT action.  
**Gap:** (1) Combat cap is -10 not -20. (2) Happiness formula ignores settlement acquisition type. (3) No War Support purchase action.  
**Recommendation:** Change combat cap to 20. Track `acquisitionType: founded|acquired|captured` on CityState. Add ADD_WAR_SUPPORT action with 180 Influence cost.

### F-09: Alliance bundle benefits -- CLOSE (missing Open Borders, Shared Visibility, Mutual Defense)

**Location:** `packages/engine/src/systems/diplomacySystem.ts:172-195`  
**GDD reference:** section "Alliances"  
**Severity:** MED  
**Effort:** M (1-3 days)  
**VII says:** Alliance at Helpful status. Benefits: Open Borders (free for both), Shared Visibility, Mutual Defense (must join or break), Sanction immunity, no Denouncements, Diplomatic Attribute capstone (+3 all yields per Alliance, repeatable).  
**Engine does:** Alliance at relationship > 60. Sets `hasAlliance: true`, +15 relationship. Blocks Denounce against ally. ResourceCalculator adds +2 Influence/alliance. No movement passability. No visibility sharing. No Mutual Defense trigger. No yield capstone.  
**Gap:** Open Borders, Shared Visibility, Mutual Defense, and the +3-all-yields capstone are all missing. The +2 Influence bonus in ResourceCalculator is also not the correct VII benefit.  
**Recommendation:** Add Open Borders implicit treaty on alliance formation. Merge ally explored tile sets on END_TURN for Shared Visibility. Add ALLIANCE_WAR_TRIGGERED notification. Add EffectDef for capstone yield bonus per alliance count.

### F-10: hasFriendship / PROPOSE_FRIENDSHIP -- EXTRA (no VII equivalent)

**Location:** `packages/engine/src/types/GameState.ts:235`, `packages/engine/src/systems/diplomacySystem.ts:198-220`  
**GDD reference:** not mentioned in VII sources  
**Severity:** LOW  
**Effort:** S (half-day)  
**VII says:** No "Declare Friendship" tier. Relationship stages (Helpful/Friendly/Neutral/Unfriendly/Hostile) are derived from opinion score. Alliances are the only formal agreement tier.  
**Engine does:** `DiplomacyRelation.hasFriendship` is a separate boolean. `PROPOSE_FRIENDSHIP` costs 0 Influence, improves relationship +20, causes +1 relationship/turn accumulation that bypasses natural decay.  
**Gap:** Custom addition with no VII equivalent. The +1/turn accumulation means relationships never naturally decay for friended pairs -- bypassing the opinion-drift mechanic.  
**Recommendation:** Remove PROPOSE_FRIENDSHIP and hasFriendship. Relationship progression toward Friendly/Helpful should be driven by Endeavors (Reconciliation) and opinion modifiers per VII design.

### F-11: Diplomatic Ledger / opinion modifier history -- MISSING

**Location:** `packages/engine/src/types/GameState.ts` (DiplomacyState)  
**GDD reference:** section "Entities -- GameState.diplomaticHistory"  
**Severity:** MED  
**Effort:** S (half-day)  
**VII says:** `GameState.diplomaticHistory` is a log for opinion modifier lookups. Relationship score is built from named modifiers (Endeavor accepted +30, Endeavor supported +60, Denounce -60, Espionage revealed large negative) that players can inspect.  
**Engine does:** `DiplomacyState` contains only `relations: ReadonlyMap<string, DiplomacyRelation>`. No history log. Relationship is a raw integer with no modifier breakdown.  
**Gap:** Players cannot inspect why a relationship is at its current value. Modifier ledger is also needed for AI decision-making.  
**Recommendation:** Add `GameState.diplomaticHistory: ReadonlyArray<DiplomaticHistoryEntry>` where each entry has `{turn, playerId, targetId, modifierType, value, label}`. Log on every opinion change.

### F-13: Peace deals -- DIVERGED (auto-accept, no bilateral, no settlement transfer)

**Location:** `packages/engine/src/systems/diplomacySystem.ts:143-169`  
**GDD reference:** section "Peace Deals"  
**Severity:** MED  
**Effort:** M (1-3 days)  
**VII says:** Peace deal scope: ONLY settlement transfers (no gold, no resources). Bilateral -- opponent must respond via Peace Deal screen. Cooldown ~10-15 turns.  
**Engine does:** PROPOSE_PEACE auto-accepts if `turnsAtWar >= 5` OR `|warSupport| <= 20`. No settlement transfer step. No peace deal modal. No cooldown. Relationship +10 on peace.  
**Gap:** (1) Auto-accept removes bilateral negotiation. (2) No settlement transfer mechanic. (3) No cooldown enforcement.  
**Recommendation:** Change PROPOSE_PEACE to create a pending peace offer. Add ACCEPT_PEACE/REJECT_PEACE for the target player. Add optional `settlementTransfers` array. Add cooldown tracking.

### F-14: Surprise war War Support delta scaling -- CLOSE

**Location:** `packages/engine/src/systems/diplomacySystem.ts:102`  
**GDD reference:** section "War Declarations"  
**Severity:** MED  
**Effort:** S (half-day)  
**VII says:** Surprise war gives the defender a War Support bonus scaling with prior relationship. Attacking a Helpful civilization is far worse than attacking an Unfriendly one.  
**Engine does:** `warSupportChange = isSurprise ? -50 : 0`. Flat -50 regardless of prior relationship. Attacking Helpful and Hostile both apply the same -50.  
**Gap:** The relationship-scaling that VII explicitly confirms is absent.  
**Recommendation:** Scale penalty by prior relationship: `penalty = 20 + max(0, round(currentRelation.relationship * 0.5))`. Gives -20 at Hostile, -50 at Friendly, -70 at Helpful -- matching the stated intent.
