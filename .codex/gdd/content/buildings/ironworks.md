## Identity

The Ironworks is the Modern Age Warehouse production building — the blast furnace and steel mill of the Industrial Revolution. It covers the broadest range of extraction improvements of any production Warehouse, including all mining and forestry types. Ageless.

- Historical period: Industrial Revolution (18th–19th century) iron and steel production
- Flavor: The blast furnace lit and the world became iron — rails, ships, and guns from one fire

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 500 Production
- Maintenance: 0 Gold (Warehouse — no upkeep)
- Yield(s): +4 Production (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Production on Mines, Quarries, Clay Pits, and Woodcutters within the settlement
- Requires: [INFERRED] Modern Age technology related to Industrialization or Steel
- Slot: Warehouse (Ageless; highest-tier extraction production Warehouse)
