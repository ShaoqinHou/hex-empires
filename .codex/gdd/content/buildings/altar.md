## Identity

The Altar is the Antiquity Age religious happiness building — a sacred space for sacrifice, prayer, or ritual. It is a prerequisite infrastructure for early faith generation and pantheon exploitation. Non-ageless: superseded by the Temple in the Exploration Age.

- Historical period: Neolithic through Iron Age sacred sites
- Flavor: Stone, fire, and the first contract between humans and gods

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 90 Production
- Maintenance: 2 Gold (no Happiness cost — unusual for happiness buildings)
- Yield(s): +2 Happiness (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Happiness per adjacent Wonder
- Requires: Mysticism (Civic)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless)
