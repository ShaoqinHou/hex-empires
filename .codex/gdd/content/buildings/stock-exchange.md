## Identity

The Stock Exchange is the high-tier Modern Age gold building — the financial market infrastructure of industrial capitalism from the Amsterdam Stock Exchange to Wall Street. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Amsterdam Stock Exchange (1602, first), NYSE (1792), London Stock Exchange
- Flavor: The price of everything, discovered simultaneously by everyone — the market as oracle
