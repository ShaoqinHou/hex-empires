## Identity

The Library is the standard Antiquity Age science building — a repository of written knowledge available to any civilization. Non-ageless but foundational: in the Antiquity Age it is the primary source of city-level Science output and is the entry-level holder of Codex slots. Superseded by the Academy (Antiquity, higher tier) and University (Exploration).

- Historical period: Ancient Near East through Greco-Roman scholarship
- Flavor: Clay tablets, papyrus scrolls, and the first scholars of the ancient world

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 90 Production
- Maintenance: 2 Happiness, 2 Gold
- Yield(s): +2 Science (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Science per adjacent Resource or Wonder tile
- Requires: Writing (Technology)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless); has 2 Codex slots
