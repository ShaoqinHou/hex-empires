## Identity

The Gristmill is the Exploration Age Warehouse food building — the mechanical evolution of the Granary. It represents water-powered and wind-powered grain milling, the backbone of medieval and early-modern food processing. Ageless — persists through all age transitions with full effects.

- Historical period: Medieval Europe, Islamic world (8th century onward), Song Dynasty China
- Flavor: The mill wheel turns, and the bread that built empires comes from its turning
