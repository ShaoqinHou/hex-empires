## Identity

The Bank is the high-tier Exploration Age gold building — representing the emergence of Renaissance-era banking institutions from the Medici to the Dutch East India Company. It provides the highest Exploration Age Gold yield and a bonus when forming a Quarter. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Medici Bank (15th century), Dutch Wisselbank, early joint-stock companies
- Flavor: The ledger as weapon — compound interest and the first financial empires

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 250 Production
- Maintenance: 3 Happiness
- Yield(s): +5 Gold (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Gold per adjacent River, Coastal, or Wonder tile
- Requires: Urban Planning (Civic)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless)
