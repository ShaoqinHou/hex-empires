## Identity

The Guildhall is a mid-tier Exploration Age gold and Influence building — the headquarters of medieval merchant and craft guilds. It unusually combines strong Gold yield with Influence, reflecting the political power of organized merchant classes in the medieval world. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Medieval European guilds (12th–15th centuries), Hanseatic League
- Flavor: The guild seal — half commerce, half covenant
