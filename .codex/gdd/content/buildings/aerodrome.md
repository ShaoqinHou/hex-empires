## Identity

The Aerodrome is the high-tier Modern Age production building focused on air power — the military airfield infrastructure required to train and deploy air units. Represents early 20th century military aviation from WWI airfields to WWII bomber bases. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: WWI military aviation (1914–1918), RAF airfields of WWII
- Flavor: The runway pointed toward the horizon — and the horizon became the battlefield
