## Identity

The City Park is the entry-level Modern Age happiness building — the public green space movement of the 19th century (Central Park, Hyde Park, Tiergarten). It is available immediately at Modern Age start with no specific tech or civic requirement. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Central Park (1858), Hyde Park, Tiergarten — the urban green space movement
- Flavor: A garden in the machine — the city's concession to the human need for grass

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 500 Production
- Maintenance: 4 Gold (no Happiness upkeep — unusual)
- Yield(s): +4 Happiness (base)
- Adjacency bonus: [INFERRED] +1 Happiness per adjacent Mountain, Natural Wonder, or Wonder
- Requires: Age Start (available at Modern Age beginning — no specific prerequisite)
