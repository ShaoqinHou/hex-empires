## Identity

The Monument is the foundational Antiquity Age culture building — a stele, column, or public artwork that asserts civic identity and builds the cultural foundation of a growing settlement. Every civ can and typically should build it as one of its earliest constructions. Non-ageless: becomes obsolete at age transition.

- Historical period: Ancient Near East through Greco-Roman antiquity
- Flavor: "A city without a monument is a village that hasn't decided who it is yet."

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 90 Production
- Maintenance: 2 Happiness, 2 Gold
- Yield(s): +2 Culture, +1 Influence
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Culture per adjacent Wonder or Mountain [INFERRED from game8.co adjacency patterns; Mountain adjacency for culture buildings is documented as standard]
- Requires: Masonry (Technology)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless)
