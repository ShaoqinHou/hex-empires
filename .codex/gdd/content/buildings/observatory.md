## Identity

The Observatory is the entry-level Exploration Age science building — replacing the Library as the primary science infrastructure of the medieval world. From the Islamic House of Wisdom's star-cataloguing to Tycho Brahe's Uraniborg, astronomical observation drove the Scientific Revolution. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Islamic observatories (Maragheh, 13th century), European astronomical towers
- Flavor: The stars as clock, compass, and proof that the universe runs on mathematics
