## Identity

The Sawmill is the Exploration Age Warehouse production building for forestry and camp industries — the powered successor to the Saw Pit. Water-powered sawmills emerged in medieval Europe and the Islamic world, dramatically accelerating timber processing. Ageless.

- Historical period: Medieval water mills (attested 13th century onward in Europe)
- Flavor: The water-wheel screams and the forest falls in controlled increments
