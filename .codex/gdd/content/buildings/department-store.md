## Identity

The Department Store is the mid-tier Modern Age happiness building — the great retail institution of industrial capitalism (Bon Marché 1852, Harrods 1849, Macy's 1858). It represents consumer culture as happiness infrastructure. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Bon Marché (Paris, 1852), Harrods (London, 1849), Selfridges (1909)
- Flavor: The abundance of things — happiness made into a shopping experience
