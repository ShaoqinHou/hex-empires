## Identity

The Kiln is the entry-level Exploration Age culture building — a firing furnace used to produce ceramics, tiles, and bricks that became cultural export goods and status markers in the medieval world. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Medieval Islamic pottery, Chinese porcelain, European tile industry
- Flavor: Blue-and-white glazed tiles, the first luxury goods of the Silk Road age
