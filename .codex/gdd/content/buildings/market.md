## Identity

The Market is the foundational Antiquity Age gold building, representing organized commercial exchange in ancient cities — the agora, the bazaar, the forum marketplace. It is the earliest way to expand a city's Resource assignment capacity. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Ancient trade centers from Mesopotamia to the Mediterranean
- Flavor: Merchants, weights, and the first money-changers of the ancient world

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 90 Production
- Maintenance: 2 Happiness (no Gold upkeep)
- Yield(s): +2 Gold (base); +1 Gold per adjacent Coastal, Lake, River, or Wonder tile
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Gold per adjacent Coastal, Lake, River, or Wonder
- Requires: Currency (Technology)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless)
