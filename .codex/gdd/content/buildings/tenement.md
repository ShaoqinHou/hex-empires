## Identity

The Tenement is the high-tier Modern Age food building — representing the dense urban housing of the Industrial Revolution. Tenements packed populations into cities to feed factory labor demand, providing high Food yield at significant social cost. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: New York Lower East Side (1840s–1900s), Victorian London slums, Glasgow tenements
- Flavor: Ten families in five rooms — the city feeds itself on its own crowding
