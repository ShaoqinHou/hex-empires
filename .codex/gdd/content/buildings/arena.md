## Identity

The Arena is the high-tier Antiquity Age happiness building — the Colosseum, the hippodrome, the stadium. It represents the Roman and Greek tradition of public spectacles as civic happiness management. The most expensive Antiquity happiness building, it provides the highest Happiness yield. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Roman amphitheaters, Greek stadia, Circus Maximus
- Flavor: Bread and circuses — the city's compact with its citizens
