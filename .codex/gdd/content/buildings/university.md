## Identity

The University is the high-tier Exploration Age science building — the institutionalized successor to the Academy, representing Bologna, Oxford, Paris, and later the great Islamic madrasas. It provides the highest Exploration Age Science yield and a quarter formation bonus. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: University of Bologna (1088), Oxford (c.1096), Paris (1150s)
- Flavor: The first institution to outlast the dynasty that funded it

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 200 Production [note: same cost as Observatory per PlayerAuctions — may reflect balance pass]
- Maintenance: 3 Happiness, 3 Gold
- Yield(s): +5 Science (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Science per adjacent Resource or Wonder tile
- Requires: Education (Technology)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless)
