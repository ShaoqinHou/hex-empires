## Identity

The Laboratory is the high-tier Modern Age science building — the dedicated research facility representing the 19th and 20th century industrial laboratory from Thomas Edison's Menlo Park to the Bell Labs era. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Edison's Menlo Park (1876), Pasteur Institute (1887), Bell Labs (1925)
- Flavor: The controlled experiment — where intuition became method and method became power

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 650 Production
- Maintenance: 4 Happiness, 4 Gold
- Yield(s): +6 Science (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Science per adjacent Resource or Wonder tile
- Requires: Electricity (Technology)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless)
