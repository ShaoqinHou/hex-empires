## Identity

The Factory is the mid-tier Modern Age production building — the defining structure of the Industrial Revolution. Steam-powered factories transformed production capacity and reshaped cities. It includes a resource slot for industrial inputs. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Industrial Revolution (1760s–1840s), Manchester textile mills, Ruhr Valley steel
- Flavor: The chimney never cools. The machine never rests. This is progress.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 600 Production
- Maintenance: 4 Happiness, 4 Gold
- Yield(s): +6 Production (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Production per adjacent Resource tile
- Requires: Mass Production (Technology); Railroad infrastructure required
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless); includes a Factory resource slot
