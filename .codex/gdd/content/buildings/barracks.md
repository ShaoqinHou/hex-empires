## Identity

The Barracks is the standard Antiquity Age military production building — a garrison facility for training and quartering soldiers. Present in almost every Civilization game, it remains the core investment for militaristic cities. Non-ageless.

- Historical period: Ancient armies from Assyrian levies to Roman legion camps
- Flavor: Drilling grounds, weapon racks, and the smell of iron — the city's fist

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Cost: 90 Production
- Maintenance: 2 Happiness, 2 Gold
- Yield(s): +2 Production (base)
- Adjacency bonus: +1 Production per adjacent Resource or Wonder tile
- Requires: Bronze Working (Technology)
- Slot: Standard urban building (non-ageless)
