## Identity

Scientific Independent Powers represent knowledge centers, ancient academies, and early universities. They align with the science victory path. Dispersing one yields 125 Science.

- Historical theme: ancient libraries, observatory cities, scholarly monasteries
- Icon: glass flask; teal/blue color coding in the UI
