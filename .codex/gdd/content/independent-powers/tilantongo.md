## Identity

Tilantongo was a major Mixtec city-state in Oaxaca, Mexico, known as a cultural and political center of the Mixtec civilization during the Classic and Post-Classic periods (roughly 600-1521 CE). It is associated with legendary ruler Eight Deer Jaguar Claw.

- Historical period: Classic to Post-Classic Mesoamerica; ~600-1521 CE
- Cultural identity: Mixtec
- Flavor: codex-producing city-state; center of Mixtec political and artistic culture
