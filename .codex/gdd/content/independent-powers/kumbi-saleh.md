## Identity

Kumbi Saleh is the believed archaeological site of Ghana (Wagadou), the capital of the Ghana Empire in present-day Mauritania/Mali. It was a major trans-Saharan trade hub from approximately the 4th through 12th centuries CE.

- Historical period: ~4th-12th century CE; Ghana Empire capital
- Cultural identity: Soninke
- Flavor: trans-Saharan gold-salt trade crossroads; walled dual city (merchant quarter + royal/spiritual quarter)
