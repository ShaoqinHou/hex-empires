## Identity

Cultural Independent Powers represent artistic centers, sacred sites, and knowledge-preserving communities. They align with the cultural victory path. Dispersing one yields 125 Culture.

- Historical theme: pilgrimage destinations, artistic cities, sacred sanctuaries
- Icon: music note or theater mask; purple/gold color coding in the UI
