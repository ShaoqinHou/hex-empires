## Identity

Samarkand is one of the oldest inhabited cities in Central Asia, located in present-day Uzbekistan. It was a key node on the Silk Road, serving as a major trading and cultural center under the Achaemenid, Hellenistic, Sogdian, and later Timurid empires.

- Historical period: ~700 BCE onwards; major Silk Road hub
- Cultural identity: Sogdian/Central Asian
- Flavor: crossroads of civilizations; center of learning and trade in the medieval Islamic world
