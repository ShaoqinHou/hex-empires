## Purpose in the game

Slotted into government policy slots (all wildcard in VII). Grant per-turn bonuses. Traditions = previous-age policies that remain selectable. Crisis Policies = unlocked during crisis.

## Category-wide rules

- All policy slots are wildcard (no economic/military/diplomatic category in VII)
- 2-3 slots initially; +1 per government adoption
- Policy swap only at civic-completion events
- Traditions = policies from past ages remain available
- Crisis Policies unlock at crisis Stage 1 (2 slots), Stage 2 (3), Stage 3 (4)
