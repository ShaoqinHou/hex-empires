## Identity

The Warrior is the starting melee unit of every civilization in the Antiquity Age — the most basic military presence on the map, representing early Bronze Age tribal fighters present from turn 1 with no technology requirement. Its low CS (20) gives players urgent incentive to research Bronze Working and upgrade to the Spearman.
