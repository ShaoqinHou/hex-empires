## Identity

The Settler is the standard civilian unit for founding new Towns during the Antiquity Age. Unlike the Founder (which founds the Capital City), Settlers found Towns — the smaller, resource-harvesting settlements of Civ VII. Settlers can be trained from the Capital City's production queue. They are essential to expansion.
