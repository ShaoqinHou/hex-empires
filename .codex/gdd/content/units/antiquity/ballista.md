## Identity

The Ballista is the Antiquity Age siege unit — a large crossbow-style weapon mounted on a wheeled carriage. It represents the earliest purpose-built siege engine, unlocked by The Wheel. It is slow (movement 1) but provides the first effective anti-fortification capability, using bombard strength against fortified districts.
