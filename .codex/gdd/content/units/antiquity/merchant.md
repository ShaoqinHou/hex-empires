## Identity

The Merchant is the Antiquity Age trade civilian unit, unlocked by Code of Laws. It establishes trade routes between cities, generating gold and other economic yields. The Merchant can enter foreign territory freely to access foreign markets, making diplomacy a prerequisite for effective trade. It moves faster (4 tiles/turn) than most land units.
