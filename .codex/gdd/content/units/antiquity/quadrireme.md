## Identity

Tier 2 (and final) Antiquity naval unit. The Quadrireme is a heavy warship — a multi-banked oared vessel with a bronze ram — representing the apex of ancient naval power. It requires Navigation and upgrades from the Galley. With its higher CS and both melee and ranged/bombard capability, it is the definitive naval unit before the Exploration Age's Cog replaces it.
