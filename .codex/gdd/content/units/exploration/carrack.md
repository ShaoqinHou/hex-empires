## Identity

Tier 2 Exploration Age naval unit. The Carrack is the large multi-masted sailing ship of the Age of Discovery — used for both trade and warfare. It requires Shipbuilding, reflecting the investment in improved maritime construction. With 4 sight range and enhanced combat capability over the Cog, it is the workhorse of mid-Exploration naval operations.
