## Identity

Tier 1 Exploration Age cavalry unit. The Courser is a fast medieval warhorse — lighter than the heavy Knight but more mobile. Available without a technology prerequisite at Exploration Age start (or auto-upgrades from Chariot). High movement (3) and ZoC immunity make it useful for early-Exploration Age raiding and flanking.
