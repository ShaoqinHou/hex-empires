## Identity

Tier 1 Exploration Age melee infantry. The Swordsman represents the transition from bronze spear-lines to iron-sword infantry at the start of the medieval period. It is the default infantry unit for any civilization without a unique Exploration Age Tier 1 infantry replacement. Available without a technology prerequisite at age start.
