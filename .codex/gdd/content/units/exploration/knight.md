## Identity

Tier 2 Exploration Age cavalry unit. The Knight is the iconic heavy medieval mounted warrior — armored horse and lance. Requires Heraldry, the civic/tech that formalizes chivalric military institutions. Stronger than the Courser with CS 45, it forms the heavy cavalry core of Exploration Age armies and is the most powerful cavalry before the Lancer.
