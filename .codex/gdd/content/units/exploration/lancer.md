## Identity

Tier 3 Exploration Age cavalry unit. The Lancer is described as "armed with matchlock pistols" despite the lance imagery — a late Renaissance pistoleer cavalryman. The most powerful cavalry unit of the Exploration Age at CS 50 with movement 3, requiring Metal Casting. It supersedes the Knight.
