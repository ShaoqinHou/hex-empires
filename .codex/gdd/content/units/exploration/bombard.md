## Identity

Tier 3 Exploration Age siege unit. The Bombard is the earliest gunpowder artillery — a large-caliber cannon capable of reducing fortified walls to rubble. Requires Gunpowder, the pivotal technology that marks the Renaissance transition. It supersedes the Trebuchet and is the most powerful siege weapon before the Modern Age's Howitzer.
