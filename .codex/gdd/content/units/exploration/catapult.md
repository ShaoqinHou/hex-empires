## Identity

Tier 1 Exploration Age siege unit. The Catapult is the early medieval siege engine — a torsion-powered rock-thrower. It replaces the Antiquity Ballista as the primary anti-fortification weapon in the first portion of the Exploration Age. Requires Machinery; slower than cavalry but essential for destroying district walls. Range 2.
