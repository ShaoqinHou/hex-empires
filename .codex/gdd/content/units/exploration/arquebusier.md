## Identity

Tier 3 Exploration Age ranged unit. The Arquebusier represents the Renaissance gunpowder infantryman — the arquebus being an early matchlock firearm. Requires Gunpowder, the same tech as the Bombard. It supersedes the Crossbowman and introduces gunpowder into the ranged line. Strong enough to threaten late-Exploration melee units.
