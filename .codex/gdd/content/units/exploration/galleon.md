## Identity

Tier 3 Exploration Age naval unit. The Galleon is the definitive warship of the Age of Discovery — the large, heavily-armed sailing warship that dominated 16th-century seas. Requires Gunpowder, making it the naval counterpart to the Arquebusier and Bombard. The most powerful Exploration naval unit before the Modern Age's steam-powered Ironclad.
