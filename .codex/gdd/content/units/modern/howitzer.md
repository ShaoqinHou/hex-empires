## Identity

Tier 3 Modern Age siege/artillery unit. The Howitzer is the WWI-era heavy artillery — a high-angle siege weapon. The most powerful bombard unit in the base game, used to reduce heavily fortified districts and city walls. Requires Industrialization. The Howitzer can devastate fortified districts with its 40 bombard strength.
