## Identity

Tier 3 Modern Age light naval unit (Destroyer class). Fast warship of the WWII era, optimized for anti-submarine warfare and fleet screening. Requires Mobilization. With CS 50 and movement 4, it is the most mobile and one of the strongest naval units in the base game. The +50% flank strength ability makes it deadly in coordinated fleet engagements.
