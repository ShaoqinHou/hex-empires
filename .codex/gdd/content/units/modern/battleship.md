## Identity

Tier 3 Modern Age heavy naval unit. The Battleship is the apex surface warship — the massive, heavily armored capital ship of the early 20th century. Requires Mobilization. With CS 55 melee and ranged 55, it is the strongest naval unit in the base game, though slower (movement 3) than the Destroyer.
