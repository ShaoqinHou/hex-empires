## Identity

Savanna woodland — scattered trees and scrub on plains terrain. Feature name is "Savanna Woodland." Represents the transitional zone between open grassland and dense forest, found across sub-Saharan Africa, Australia, and South America. Surprisingly high Production with no Food component, unlike the Grassland forest variant.

- Historical period: African savanna woodland, Australian bushland, South American cerrado
- Flavor: Sparse woodland on dry plains; production-heavy, no food offset.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +3 Production
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (vegetated modifier)
- Defense modifier: +2 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Sparse vegetation provides +2 CS defensive cover
- Can build: Woodcutter/Lumber Mill (auto-improvement), Roads reduce to 1 MP
