## Identity

Open temperate grassland with no elevation, vegetation, or wetland. The highest raw Food terrain in the game. Historically associated with agricultural heartlands and early farming settlements along temperate latitudes. Wide flat meadows ideal for cultivation.

- Historical period: Temperate agricultural zones worldwide
- Flavor: Premier food-producing base tile; the farmland archetype.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +3 Food
- Movement cost: 1 MP (flat modifier is always 1 MP)
- Defense modifier: 0
- Combat rules: No special rules; standard unit entry and combat
- Can build: Farm (auto-improvement), Quarters, Roads; general improvements allowed
