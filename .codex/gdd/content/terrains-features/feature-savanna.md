## Identity

Savanna Woodland — the "Plains Vegetated" biome modifier. Scattered acacia trees and scrub on dry grassland. Represents the African savanna, Australian bushland, and South American cerrado. Pure Production tile — the dry climate limits Food but trees provide timber. Highest Production of any vegetated tile.

- Historical period: Sub-Saharan Africa, Australia, South American cerrado, Indian dry deciduous forest
- Flavor: Open woodland on dry plains; maximum production among vegetated tiles.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +3 Production
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (vegetated modifier)
- Defense modifier: +2 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Sparse tree cover provides +2 CS defensive cover
- Can build: Woodcutter/Lumber Mill (auto-improvement), Roads
