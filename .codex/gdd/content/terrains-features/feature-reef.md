## Identity

Reef — a marine feature appearing on Coastal tiles (not Lakes, per 1.3.0 patch). Coral reefs represent some of the most productive marine ecosystems. In Civ VII, Reefs add yield value to workable coastal tiles. Atolls (deep ocean variant) are movement-stopping and provide a combat defense bonus for naval units.

- Historical period: Tropical and subtropical coral reef ecosystems
- Flavor: Productive coastal feature; marine food and gold source.
