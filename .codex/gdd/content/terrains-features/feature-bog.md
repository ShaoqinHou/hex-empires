## Identity

Bog — the "Tundra Wet" biome modifier. Waterlogged peat bog in arctic or subarctic terrain. Represents the permafrost bogs of Siberia, northern Canada, and Scandinavia — vast peat accumulations with archaeological and fuel value. Unique three-yield tile (Food, Production, Culture) in the Tundra biome.

- Historical period: Siberian peat bogs, Scandinavian mires, Canadian muskeg
- Flavor: Ancient peat land with unexpected cultural richness; movement-stopping but diversely productive.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +1 Food, +2 Production, +1 Culture
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (wet modifier)
- Defense modifier: 0 [INFERRED]
- Combat rules: Movement-stopping; no combat advantage
- Can build: Limited wet/bog-compatible improvements
