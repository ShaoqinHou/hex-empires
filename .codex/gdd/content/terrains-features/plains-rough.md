## Identity

Hilly plains — rolling hills in a semi-arid biome. The highest raw Production tile among non-tropical land biomes. Represents the rugged terrain of semi-arid uplands where mineral extraction dominates land use. Excellent for production-focused civilizations and mining economies.

- Historical period: Semi-arid hill country; mining and pastoral uplands
- Flavor: Maximum production output; premier mining terrain.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +3 Production
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (rough modifier)
- Defense modifier: +3 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Defender gains +3 CS; attacker expends all MP entering
- Can build: Mine (auto-improvement), Quarry where stone present, Roads
