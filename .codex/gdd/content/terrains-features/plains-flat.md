## Identity

Open flat plains — semi-arid grassland with less moisture than Grassland biome. Slightly lower Food than Grassland Flat but adds Production, making it a balanced early-game tile. Represents the great temperate steppes, American Great Plains, and Eurasian steppe belt.

- Historical period: Semi-arid temperate plains; pastoral and light agriculture zones
- Flavor: Balanced food/production; slightly drier than Grassland.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +2 Food, +1 Production
- Movement cost: 1 MP (flat modifier)
- Defense modifier: 0
- Combat rules: No special rules; standard unit entry
- Can build: Farm (auto-improvement), Quarters, Roads, general improvements
