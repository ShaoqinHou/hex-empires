## Identity

Tropical hills — elevated terrain in equatorial regions. Represents the volcanic highland zones of Central America, East Africa, and Southeast Asian archipelagos. Balances Food and Production with the standard rough-modifier defense bonus. Overlaps thematically with tropical volcanic uplands.

- Historical period: Central American highlands, East African rift highlands, Southeast Asian volcanic uplands
- Flavor: Steep tropical hillsides; food and production with defensive advantage.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +1 Food, +2 Production
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (rough modifier)
- Defense modifier: +3 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Defender gains +3 CS; movement depleted on entry
- Can build: Mine (auto-improvement), Plantation (where resources), Roads
