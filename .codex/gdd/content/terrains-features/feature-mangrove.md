## Identity

Mangrove — the "Tropical Wet" biome modifier. Coastal tropical wetland with flooded root systems. The only wet terrain with a Science component, mirroring Rainforest's Science yield in the same biome. Mangroves serve as nursery ecosystems for marine life and are uniquely productive. Found along tropical coastlines globally.

- Historical period: Tropical coastal wetlands; Southeast Asia, West Africa, Caribbean, Amazon estuary
- Flavor: Coastal jungle swamp; the triple-yield wet tile of the tropical frontier.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +1 Food, +2 Production, +1 Science
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (wet modifier)
- Defense modifier: 0 [INFERRED]
- Combat rules: Movement-stopping; no combat advantage
- Can build: Limited coastal/wet-compatible improvements
