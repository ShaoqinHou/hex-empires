## Identity

Coastal water tile — shallow near-shore marine terrain workable by settled cities. Part of the Marine biome, distinct from the deep ocean. Represents the productive coastal fishing grounds and trade lanes that powered maritime civilizations. Supports Fishing Boats and coastal buildings.

- Historical period: Global coastal zones; primary marine economic terrain
- Flavor: Shallow coastal waters; the workhorse of maritime economies.
