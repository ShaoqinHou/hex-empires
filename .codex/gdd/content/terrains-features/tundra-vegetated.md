## Identity

Taiga — boreal conifer forest in the tundra biome. Feature name is "Taiga." Represents the vast boreal forest belt of Siberia, northern Canada, and Scandinavia. Unique in yielding Culture, making it the only base terrain with a Culture component. Mirrors the Tropical biome Science yield, giving cold civilizations a cultural advantage.

- Historical period: Siberian taiga, Canadian boreal forest, Scandinavian pine forests
- Flavor: Ancient forest yielding cultural heritage; the north's cultural heartland.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +2 Production, +1 Culture
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (vegetated modifier)
- Defense modifier: +2 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Forest cover provides +2 CS defensive bonus; movement depleted on entry
- Can build: Woodcutter/Lumber Mill (auto-improvement), Roads
