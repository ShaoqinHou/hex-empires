## Identity

Forest — the "Grassland Vegetated" biome modifier. Temperate deciduous and mixed woodland. The classic Civ forest tile, now integrated into the biome-modifier system. Represents the great temperate forests of northern Europe, eastern North America, and northern Asia. Balanced Food and Production with defensive cover.

- Historical period: Temperate deciduous and mixed woodland zones globally
- Flavor: Dense but navigable woodland; balanced production with defensive advantage.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +1 Food, +2 Production
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (vegetated modifier)
- Defense modifier: +2 Combat Strength (defender only)
- Combat rules: Forest cover +2 CS to defender; movement depleted on entry
- Can build: Woodcutter/Lumber Mill (auto-improvement), Roads
