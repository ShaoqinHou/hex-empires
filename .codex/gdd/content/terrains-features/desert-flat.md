## Identity

Flat desert terrain — arid open land with minimal vegetation. Represents the great sandy deserts (Sahara, Arabian, Gobi flat sections). Lowest Food production biome but still viable as a starter given Civ VII design philosophy that all biomes are viable. Gold and Production are more available in this biome via other modifiers.

- Historical period: Arid flat desert environments worldwide
- Flavor: Barren open desert; viable with trade and resource bonuses.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +2 Food, +1 Production
- Movement cost: 1 MP (flat modifier)
- Defense modifier: 0
- Combat rules: No special rules; standard unit entry
- Can build: Farm (auto-improvement where resources allow), Wells, Roads
