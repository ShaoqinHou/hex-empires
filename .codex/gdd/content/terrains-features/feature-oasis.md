## Identity

Oasis — the "Desert Wet" biome modifier. A water source in the arid desert providing Food, Production, and Gold. In Civ VII the oasis is integrated as the wet modifier of the Desert biome rather than a standalone feature overlay. Historically the foundation of desert trade route civilizations from Palmyra to Timbuktu.

- Historical period: Saharan, Arabian, and Central Asian oasis trading posts
- Flavor: Desert jewel; food, production, and gold in one scarce tile.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +1 Food, +2 Production, +1 Gold
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (wet modifier)
- Defense modifier: 0 [INFERRED]
- Combat rules: Movement-stopping wet terrain; no combat bonus
- Can build: Wells, limited desert-wet improvements; potential fresh water settlement
