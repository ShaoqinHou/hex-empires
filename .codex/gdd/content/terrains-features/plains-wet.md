## Identity

Watering hole — a rare wet patch in the semi-arid Plains biome. Feature name is "Watering Hole." Represents oasis-adjacent pools, ephemeral lakes, and seasonal floodplains in dry grassland environments. Highest Production+Food in the Plains biome but movement-stopping.

- Historical period: African and Australian water sources; semi-arid wetland pockets
- Flavor: Life-giving water pocket in dry plains; high yields, slow entry.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +1 Food, +3 Production
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (wet modifier)
- Defense modifier: 0 [INFERRED]
- Combat rules: Movement-stopping entry; no confirmed defense bonus for wet modifier
- Can build: Auto-improvement limited to wet-compatible options; Roads reduce to 1 MP
