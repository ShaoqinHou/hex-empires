## Identity

Deep ocean — the vast open sea separating Homelands from Distant Lands. Impassable until the Exploration Age tech path is completed. Damages non-capable units with attrition. Becomes workable only in the Modern Age. Unique mechanic in Civ VII, separating the two continental zones as a physical barrier mirroring the game's age structure.

- Historical period: Represents pre-Columbian oceans — the barrier to Distant Lands
- Flavor: The great unknown; dangerous barrier that becomes an economic highway in the Modern Age.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +1 Food, +3 Gold (Modern Age only; unworkable before Modern)
- Movement cost: Depletes all remaining MP (acts as difficult terrain); 11-20 HP attrition per turn without ocean-crossing tech in Exploration Age
- Defense modifier: 0 [INFERRED]
- Combat rules: Non-naval units cannot enter; naval units take attrition until full tech path completed
- Can build: No improvements on ocean tiles
