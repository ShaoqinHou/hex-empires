## Identity

Inland lake — freshwater body within the Marine biome. Often connected to the coast via navigable rivers. Provides fresh water to adjacent settlements and is workable by cities. Lotus feature (added in 1.3.0) can appear on lake tiles, replacing the earlier Reef placement there.

- Historical period: Inland freshwater bodies globally; Great Lakes analogs
- Flavor: Inland fresh water; strategic settlement resource providing happiness bonus.
