## Identity

Desert oasis — a water source in arid terrain providing Food, Production, and Gold. Feature name is "Oasis." Historically the lifeline of desert trade routes; oasis cities like Palmyra and Timbuktu emerged as trade hubs precisely because of these rare fertile zones. The most economically valuable wet terrain in the game.

- Historical period: Saharan, Arabian, and Central Asian oasis trade centers
- Flavor: The desert jewel; rare water source yielding Food, Production, and Gold.

## Stats / numeric attributes

- Base yield: +1 Food, +2 Production, +1 Gold
- Movement cost: Deplete all remaining MP (wet modifier)
- Defense modifier: 0 [INFERRED]
- Combat rules: Movement-stopping entry; no confirmed defense bonus
- Can build: Limited to oasis-compatible improvements; Wells; potential fresh water settlement bonus
