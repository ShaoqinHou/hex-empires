## Identity

- Historical period: Friedrich II (Frederick the Great) of Prussia, 1712-1786; philosopher-king and military commander
- Flavor: The Baroque persona captures Friedrich as a patron of the arts - an Enlightenment monarch who commissioned concerts and built ornate palaces while also leading military campaigns. DLC variant (Founders Deluxe Content Pack).
- Persona variants: Oblique (base game)
- DLC status: Founders Deluxe Content Pack

## Stats / numeric attributes

For leaders:
- Attributes: Cultural (primary), Militaristic (secondary)
- Agenda: Parisian Sensibilities - Increase Relationship per Wonder built in the Capital by a Medium Amount; Decrease Relationship per Building (non-Wonder) in the Capital by a Small Amount.
- Unique ability: Hohenfriedberger Marsch - Gain a Great Work upon capturing a Settlement for the first time; Gain an Infantry Unit when constructing a Culture Building.
- Preferred civs: Egypt (Antiquity - Great Work synergy); Greece (Antiquity alternative); Norman or Spain (Exploration); Prussia or France (Modern)
- Starting bias: None
