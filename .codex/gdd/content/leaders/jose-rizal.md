## Identity

- Historical period: 1861-1896; Filipino nationalist, novelist, and revolutionary martyr executed by Spanish colonial authorities
- Flavor: Rizal represents the pen-over-sword tradition of nationalist movements - a reformist who inspired revolution through writing rather than arms. His design rewards Narrative Events, Celebrations, and diplomatic cooperation.
- Persona variants: None

## Stats / numeric attributes

For leaders:
- Attributes: Cultural (primary), Diplomatic (secondary)
- Agenda: Kapwa - Increase Relationship by a Medium Amount with the player starting the most Endeavors; Decrease Relationship by a Medium Amount with the player starting the most Sanctions.
- Unique ability: Pambansang Bayani - When gaining rewards from a Narrative Event, gain +20 Culture and Gold per Age; 50% Increased Celebration duration and Happiness toward Celebrations; Has additional Narrative Events available.
- Preferred civs: Hawaii (Exploration - extra happiness from fishing boats accelerates celebrations); any happiness or culture-focused civ
- Starting bias: Tropical
