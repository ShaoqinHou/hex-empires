## Stats / numeric attributes

For leaders:
- Attributes: Economic (primary), Expansionist (secondary)
- Agenda: Wonderlust - Decrease Relationship significantly for each Natural Wonder within another player borders; Increase Relationship slightly if a player controls no Natural Wonders.

- Starting bias: Coast, Natural Wonders
