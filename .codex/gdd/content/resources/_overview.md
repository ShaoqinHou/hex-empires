## Purpose in the game

Bonus / Luxury / Strategic. Assigned to settlements for empire-wide effects (NOT worked on tiles as in VI). Limited slots per settlement. New resources appear per age.

## Category-wide rules

- Three types: Bonus, Luxury, Strategic
- Assigned to settlements for bonuses (VII: per-settlement assignment, not tile-work as VI)
- Age-gated: new resources appear at each age transition
- Trade Routes import resources from partners
- Empire resource cap (base + civic/tech unlocks)
- Distant Lands exclusive resources (5): chocolate, tea, spices + 2 unknown
